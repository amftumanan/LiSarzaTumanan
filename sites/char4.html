Who IS this character?


  • Name: Kasane (重音) - Multiple meaning "Piled-up Sound," "Double Sound," "Heavy Sound." Teto (テト) - Shortened from Tetopettenson, a parody song of Le Beau Tambour.
  • Type: BOUCALOID (某CALOID) - Roughly translated as Vo-kinda. This is a play on words. 某caloid has the same pronunciation as Vocaloid in Japanese, and means "so-and-so-caloid," i.e., a fake Vocaloid for an April Fool's joke.
