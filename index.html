Carefully Characterizing Character Qualities

by Jaycee Sarza and Abren Tumanan

Carefully Characterizing Character Qualities functions similarly to a wiki page. The home page contains the links which each lead to the webpages containing the main body of the website. Each webpage details a fictional character of our choosing. Information that can be found on the webpage may include: work of origin, description of appearance and behaviour, relevance of character to the plot of the work of origin, why people like this character, and why the problems they are face say about the situation of people around the world.
